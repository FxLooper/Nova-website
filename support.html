Frequently asked questions

Does Nova send my voice to a cloud service?
No. Nova connects only to your own backend server, which you set up and control. There is no "Nova cloud" we operate. The wake word ("Hey Nova") runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple's on-device speech recognition.
Can I try Nova without a backend server?
Yes. Use Demo / Guest mode on the welcome screen. In demo mode the app makes no network connections — you can explore the UI, the orb, and the wake word locally.
How do I connect my own Nova backend?
During onboarding, enter your backend URL and authentication token. The app stores these in the iOS Keychain. The backend can run on a home server, a VPS, or behind Tailscale — Nova will reach it as long as the URL is reachable from your iPhone.
The wake word doesn't trigger. What can I do?
Make sure microphone permission is granted in Settings → Nova → Microphone. The wake word also requires the app to be in the foreground or to have run in the background recently. If you have a heavy accent, try the alternative phrases ("Hi Nova", "Ahoj Nova", "Ok Nova") or re-record your voice profile in Voice ID settings.
Voice ID doesn't recognize me.
Re-enroll your voice from the Voice ID screen. Speak the enrollment phrases in a quiet environment, holding the phone the way you normally would. The app uses anti-spoofing checks, so playing back a recording of yourself will not authenticate.
How big a video can I upload?
Up to 200 MB per upload. Larger files will be rejected client-side before the upload starts.
Why does the app need my location?
Only for queries that explicitly need it — weather, "what's near me", local search. Location is sent only when in use, only when relevant, and never written to any local log.
Does Nova work offline?
Wake word detection and demo mode work offline. Anything that requires Claude (the language model) needs network access to your backend, which in turn needs to reach the model provider.
How do I delete my data?
Delete the app to remove everything stored on your iPhone (settings, keychain, conversation cache). Server-side data lives on your backend — you control retention there.
Can I use Nova without speaking aloud?
Yes. Tap the keyboard icon to type instead of speaking. All conversational features work in text mode.

Reporting a bug

When emailing about a bug, please include:

  • iPhone model and iOS version
  • Nova app version (Settings → About)
  • What you did, what you expected, what actually happened
  • Screenshot or screen recording if possible

Feature requests

We read every email. If there's something you want Nova to do, tell us — small ideas and big ones both welcome.
